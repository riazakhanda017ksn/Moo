Design guidelines

Design Guidelines

Bleed Area: 3.66" x 2.16"

Make sure that your background extends to fill the bleed to avoid your Business Cards having white edges when trimmed.

Trim: 3.50" x 2.0"

This is where we aim to cut your cards.

Safe Area: 3.34" x 1.84"

Make sure any important parts of your Business Card design (eg text and logos) are inside of the safe area, otherwise they may be cut off.



Special Finish Artwork

To create standard-size Business Cards with a special finish, you'll need two files. One for the artwork, one for the special finish. The artwork file will house the main design image. Use a CMYK color file in PDF, PNG, JPG, or GIF format. The special finish file will define where it's applied on top of your design. Use a black and white PDF.

What's the recommended standard Business Card size in pixels?

If you're uploading photography or non-vector artwork (eg high-quality JPEGs) for your standard-size Business Cards, make sure it's no less than 352 x 208 pixels at 96dpi (or ideally 1098 x 648 pixels at 300dpi).
